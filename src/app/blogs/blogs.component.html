Trail Leader:
Responsibilities: Plans and leads hikes, ensures trails are suitable for the group, checks weather conditions, and keeps track of the group's pace.
Safety Officer:
Responsibilities: Ensures the safety of all members during hikes, carries first aid kit, knows basic first aid, and maintains communication in case of emergencies.
Logistics Coordinator:
Responsibilities: Organizes transportation, schedules meet-up times and locations, ensures everyone has necessary gear, and manages any permits or fees.
Communications Officer:
Responsibilities: Manages the group's social media, sends out emails or messages about upcoming hikes, and updates members on any changes or news.
Gear Manager:
Responsibilities: Keeps track of group gear and equipment, ensures gear is maintained and in good condition, and coordinates borrowing or renting of gear.
EventModel Planner:
Responsibilities: Plans social events for the group outside of hiking, such as potlucks, training sessions, or educational workshops about hiking and nature.
